Allume

Taking the work out of shopping, helping women find clothes that bring out their inner-spark.

Allume believes that shopping is better with a bestie - or in their case, a stylist. Allume stylists are there to help users find clothes that feel personalized and unique, all while saving time and having fun.

Allume's goal is to to take the work out of shopping, and find people clothes that bring out their inner-spark.

The challenge

Real women with full lives who love to shop often don’t have time to keep up with the latest fashions. Allume matches up users with a stylist to assist them with assembling flattering unique looks for casual, formal, and business events.

The Solution

UXPros built upon Allume’s existing style guide and requirements with a playful interactive quiz to evaluate users’ fashion preferences.

Our Role

Working with Allume’s Marketing team, we created a comprehensive set of wireframes and a working prototype to proof the concept and enable a smooth handoff to the development team.
